FAQ

Is this software or an automated tool?

No. This is a prompt-driven compliance operating system. You manually run prompts in an AI tool to generate draft documents.

Does this generate documents automatically?

No. You copy a prompt, run it in ChatGPT (or similar), answer questions, then review and approve the draft output.

Why is the system view-only?

It’s view-only to protect the framework. You copy the pages you need into your own workspace and manage your operational documents privately.

What is B6 verification and tracking?

B6 generates inspection logs, training records, maintenance logs, corrective action trackers, and other proof-of-implementation records.

What’s the difference between Tier 2 and Tier 3?

Tier 2 is for one operation. Tier 3 adds multi-site licensing plus governance tools like readiness assessment, audit tracking, and version control.

Is this legal advice or a compliance guarantee?

No. Draft documentation only. Employers remain responsible for review, approval, implementation, training, and ongoing compliance.

What support is included?

Support is limited to access and system-use clarification only. No consulting, document review, or inspector communication.
