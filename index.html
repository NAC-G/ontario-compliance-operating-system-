How the System Works

You use the generators to create draft compliance documents. The system is delivered in Notion as a view-only workspace. You copy what you need into your own workspace, run prompts in an AI tool, and implement internally.

1. Choose the generator (B1–B6)
2. Copy the prompt into ChatGPT (or similar)
3. Answer questions about your operation
4. Review, approve, implement, and track

AI drafts the documentation. You remain responsible for accuracy, approval, training, and implementation.

FAQ

Clear answers to reduce confusion and prevent mismatched expectations.

Is this software or an automated tool?

No. This is a prompt-driven compliance operating system. You manually run prompts in an AI tool to generate draft documents.

Does this generate documents automatically?

No. You copy a prompt, run it in ChatGPT (or similar), answer questions, then review and approve the draft output.

Why is the system view-only?

It’s view-only to protect the framework. You copy the pages you need into your own workspace and manage your operational documents privately.

What is B6 verification and tracking?

B6 helps you generate inspection logs, training records, maintenance logs, corrective action trackers, and other proof-of-implementation records.

Is this legal advice or a compliance guarantee?

No. This system provides draft documentation only. Employers remain responsible for review, approval, implementation, training, and ongoing compliance.
