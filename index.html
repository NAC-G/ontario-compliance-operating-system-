Sample SOP — Portable Grinder (Draft) Preview • Draft structure example • Employer must review/approve 
DOCUMENT ID: SOP-EQP-001 (Draft)
TITLE: Safe Operating Procedure — Portable Grinder
SCOPE: Applies to workers using portable grinders for cutting, grinding, or finishing tasks.

1. PURPOSE
To provide a structured, repeatable procedure that reduces risk of injury and supports consistent worker training.

2. REQUIRED PPE
- CSA-approved eye protection and face shield (as required)
- Hearing protection (as required)
- Gloves suitable for task
- Proper footwear (non-slip, safety toe as required)

3. HAZARDS
- Flying debris and sparks
- Laceration risk from wheel contact
- Kickback and loss of control
- Noise exposure
- Fire risk near combustibles

4. PRE-USE CHECKS
- Verify guard is installed and secure
- Confirm wheel condition (no cracks, chips)
- Confirm correct wheel type for material and RPM rating
- Ensure work area is clear of combustibles
- Verify cord/plug integrity (if corded)

5. SAFE OPERATING STEPS
1) Secure workpiece (clamp or stable surface)
2) Maintain two-hand control and stable stance
3) Start tool before contacting workpiece
4) Apply light, controlled pressure; do not force wheel
5) Direct sparks away from people and combustibles
6) Stop tool before setting down

6. STOP WORK CONDITIONS
Stop work immediately if:
- Guard becomes loose/damaged
- Wheel is cracked or abnormal vibration occurs
- Sparks ignite materials or smoke is observed

7. TRAINING & VERIFICATION
- Worker receives orientation on hazards and safe steps
- Supervisor verifies competence (sign-off)

APPROVAL (Employer)
Prepared By: __________  Date: __________
Approved By: __________  Date: __________

How the System Works

You use the generators to create draft compliance documents. The system is delivered in Notion as a view-only workspace. You copy what you need into your own workspace, run prompts in an AI tool, and implement internally.

1. Choose the generator (B1–B6)
2. Copy the prompt into ChatGPT (or similar)
3. Answer questions about your operation
4. Review, approve, implement, and track

AI drafts the documentation. You remain responsible for accuracy, approval, training, and implementation.
