What Is OCOS

Compliance infrastructure,
not a document bundle.

The Ontario Compliance Operating System™ is a structured, three-tier compliance architecture that takes an Ontario employer from legal orientation to a fully operational, inspection-ready safety program — without starting from scratch, relying on generic templates, or engaging consultants for every document update.

It is built on the Occupational Health and Safety Act R.S.O. 1990, c. O.1 and its regulations. Every document, framework, and workflow within the system is designed to produce defensible evidence of due diligence — not paperwork for its own sake.

Documents sitting in a binder are not a compliance program. Evidence that a system operated — is.

OCOS Design Principle
Pillar 01
Inspection-Ready Structure

Organized, controlled, and version-tracked documentation that can be produced on demand during an MOL inspection, WSIB audit, or legal proceeding — without scrambling.

Pillar 02
Prompt-Driven Generation

AI-powered document generation using the proprietary RCCF framework. Produce tailored policies, procedures, and programs from your actual operational details — in minutes, not months.

Pillar 03
Tracking & Verification

Training registers, inspection logs, corrective action records, and a compliance calendar that convert written documents into a live, operating safety program with a traceable evidence chain.
