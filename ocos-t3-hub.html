Activation

Activating Tier 3

1
Confirm Tier 1 and Tier 2 are complete

Tier 3 is only activated after the Foundation Guide has been reviewed and signed (T1), and at least one initial document suite has been generated and approved through Tier 2. Do not activate Tier 3 with no approved documents to manage.

Prerequisite check
2
Read the Tier 3 Guide

Open the Compliance Operations System Guide above. Pay particular attention to the Document Control Register setup, training record requirements, and the minimum retention periods for each record type.

Before opening Notion workspace
3
Open the 👋 Start Here guide in Notion

In your Tier 3 Notion workspace, open the Start Here setup guide. It walks through the initial workspace configuration — setting up your document register, populating the compliance calendar, and filing your first approved documents from Tier 2.

First 30 minutes in Notion
4
File your first approved documents

Transfer your Tier 2-approved documents into the Document Control Register. Assign effective dates, version numbers, and review dates. This activates the system and starts your compliance record.

Upon first document approval
5
Maintain the system on an ongoing basis

Tier 3 is not a one-time setup. It is maintained continuously — inspection records filed after each shift, training records updated after each orientation, and corrective actions tracked to close-out. A compliance system that stops being maintained stops being defensible.

Ongoing — every operational day
