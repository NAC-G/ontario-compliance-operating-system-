What It Is

Not an app.
The NAC OS Fleet Logbook.

NAC OS Fleet Logbook™ is a self-contained browser-based application — no installation, no Google account required, no subscription software. Data is stored locally in your browser. Works on any device. Deploy by hosting one HTML file.

QR codes per machine open the daily log instantly. OCR meter capture auto-fills hours from a photo. PM scheduling tracks every service interval by both hours and calendar days. Everything connects — daily logs feed timesheets, timesheets feed reports, service records feed the PM schedule.

The operator scans the QR code in the cab. The meter photo updates the hours. The daily log is complete before they leave the machine.

How It Works

Scan → Log →
Track → Report.

1
Setup
Deploy the app and configure machines

Host the Fleet Logbook HTML file. Add your machines in Fleet Assets. Generate QR codes per machine — one URL per unit, paste in the cab. Enter your license key in Settings. Takes one afternoon.

Fleet Assets
2
Daily — Shift Start
Operator scans QR, photographs meter, runs pre-use checks

Operator scans the QR in the cab — Dashboard or Daily Log opens. Photographs the hour meter — OCR fills Start Hours automatically. Runs through the pre-use checklist on their phone. Shift is logged before the first bucket moves.

QR + OCR
3
Daily — Shift End
End hours, fuel, materials, issues logged. Supervisor signs off.

End of shift: operator photographs meter again, logs fuel added, materials used, any issues or corrective actions. Supervisor sign-off captured. Hours Used calculates automatically. Timesheet row linked.

Equipment Log
4
Ongoing
PM alerts flag service items. Service records logged. Reports auto-generate.

Service Alerts tab updates automatically as hours accumulate. When an item hits DUE SOON or OVERDUE, it surfaces immediately. Log the service — part number, cost, who did it — and the PM schedule resets. Monthly reports generate without any manual work.

PM + Reports
