Opt Out

This page explains how you can opt out of marketing communications and certain tracking/advertising practices.

1) Marketing email opt-out

If you receive marketing emails from us, you can opt out at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the email or by emailing info@naturalalternatives.ca with the subject line “Marketing Opt Out”.

Transactional messages (such as purchase confirmations or access instructions) may still be sent because they are necessary to deliver the product or respond to support requests.

2) Cookies and analytics controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you block or delete cookies, some site features may not work as expected.

  • Block or limit third-party cookies in your browser.
  • Clear stored data/cookies for this site.
  • If a cookie banner is enabled, set preferences there.

See also: Cookie Policy

3) Interest-based advertising opt-out

If third-party advertising or remarketing tools are used, you may be able to opt out of interest-based ads through:

  • Your device or browser ad settings (where available).
  • Industry opt-out tools provided by ad networks (availability varies by region and provider).

We do not control third-party opt-out mechanisms. For more detail, see: Third Party Online Advertising.

4) Contact

If you have questions about opt-out choices or privacy, contact us at info@naturalalternatives.ca.
