Getting Started

Tier 2 Generation — First Steps

1
Read the Tier 2 Guide

Open the AI-Driven Compliance Generation Framework Guide above. Read Sections 3 and 4 — the RCCF Framework and Company Master Prompt — before opening your Notion workspace.

Before opening Notion
2
Complete your Company Master Prompt

In the Notion workspace, open the Company Master Prompt template. Fill in your business name, sector, geographic area, workforce size, equipment inventory, hazard profile, and applicable regulations. Every generation session starts with this prompt.

First session setup — approximately 30 minutes
3
Select a generator from the Compliance Library

Open the Compliance Generator Library in Notion. Select the document you need — Health & Safety Policy, Safe Work Procedure, Inspection Checklist, or others. Paste the Master Prompt, then the generator prompt, into a new AI session.

One generator per session
4
Review every output before approving

Generated documents are drafts. Verify that all hazard descriptions, control measures, equipment references, and role assignments reflect your actual workplace before signing and issuing any document.

Mandatory — every document
5
Transfer approved documents to Tier 3

Reviewed and approved documents are filed, managed, and maintained through the Tier 3 Compliance Operations System. Tier 2 generates. Tier 3 operates.

Upon employer sign-off

A well-formatted AI-generated document that has not been reviewed, approved, communicated, and enforced provides no legal protection to the employer and no safety benefit to the worker. The format is not the control. The implementation is the control.
