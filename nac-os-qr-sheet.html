Operator — How to log your shift
1Scan the QR code posted in the cab of your machine.
2The log entry form opens on your phone. Machine is pre-selected.
3Enter your name, start / end hour meter readings, and fuel added.
4Complete the pre-use checklist — tap OK, Monitor, or Fix for each item.
5Add any notes on issues or corrective actions. Tap Submit.
If the QR code doesn't scan
→Clean the QR code surface — dirt or glare can block scanning.
→Move to better lighting or try shade if in direct sun.
→Open your camera app and hold steady 20–30 cm from the code.
→Contact your fleet manager if the problem continues.
