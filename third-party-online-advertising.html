Third Party Online Advertising

This disclosure explains how third-party advertising and measurement tools may be used and what choices you have.

1) What “third-party online advertising” means

Third-party advertising typically refers to ad networks or platforms that help display ads, measure ad performance, or enable remarketing (showing ads to people who previously visited a website).

These providers may use cookies, pixels, SDKs, or similar technologies to collect information such as your device type, browser details, IP address, and pages visited. This information helps measure campaign effectiveness and, if enabled, personalize ads.

2) How we may use advertising tools

Depending on how this site is configured, we may use advertising/measurement tools to:

  • Measure conversions (e.g., purchases, upgrade clicks, form submissions).
  • Understand which pages perform best and improve user experience.
  • Run limited remarketing campaigns to reach previous visitors (if enabled).

If you do not run ads or tracking, this page remains a transparency disclosure and your deployment may have minimal or no advertising cookies.

3) Your choices and opt-out options

You can often reduce tracking or personalized advertising by:

  • Using browser settings to block third-party cookies and clear site data.
  • Adjusting device ad settings (where available).
  • Using opt-out tools offered by advertising platforms or industry programs (availability varies).

For steps you can take right now, see: Opt Out and Cookie Policy.

4) No sale of personal information (typical meaning)

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense. If advertising tools are enabled, third parties may still collect certain technical identifiers for ad measurement or delivery purposes. Where required, we aim to use reasonable consent and disclosure practices.

5) Contact

Questions about advertising disclosures can be sent to info@naturalalternatives.ca.
