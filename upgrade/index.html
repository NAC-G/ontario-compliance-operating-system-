Tier Overview

Tier 1 — Compliance Foundation

Tier 2 — Compliance Generator Library

Best for employers who need structured prompts to generate draft documents.

  • Prompt-driven document drafting (B1–B5)
  • Repeatable regeneration when operations change
  • Examples (structure only)

Best for employers who require governance, verification, and lifecycle control.

  • Governance & oversight layer
  • Verification & tracking (B6)
