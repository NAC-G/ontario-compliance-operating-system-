Ontario/Canada privacy-aligned notice • Plain-language

1) Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how Natural Alternatives (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal information in connection with the Ontario Compliance Operating System™ website and related services. We operate in Ontario, Canada.

This policy is intended to align with commonly applicable Canadian privacy principles (including PIPEDA-style fair information principles) and Ontario expectations. It is not legal advice.

2) What personal information we collect

Depending on how you interact with the site, we may collect:

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email) if you contact us or request support.
  • Transaction-related information (e.g., purchase confirmation details) if you buy a tier. Payments are processed by third-party processors (e.g., Stripe).
  • Technical and usage data (e.g., IP address, device type, browser, pages viewed, referral URLs) through logs and cookies/analytics (if enabled).
  • Preference data (e.g., marketing opt-out choices or cookie preferences).

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information through this marketing site. If you choose to email us, do not include sensitive details unless necessary.

3) How we use personal information

We use personal information for purposes that a reasonable person would consider appropriate, including:

  • Providing access, support, and responding to inquiries.
  • Processing purchases and delivering purchase-related communications.
  • Maintaining site security and preventing fraud or misuse.
  • Understanding how the site is used and improving content and conversion flows (analytics).
  • Sending marketing communications only where permitted and with appropriate opt-out options.

4) Consent and your choices

We obtain consent where required and provide choices, including the ability to opt out of marketing communications. For cookies and analytics, consent mechanisms may be implemented depending on your deployment configuration.

You can opt out of marketing at any time using the instructions in our Opt Out page.

5) How we share information

We may share personal information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the site and deliver services (e.g., hosting, email, analytics).
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) to process payments. We do not store full payment card details on our servers.
  • Legal or regulatory authorities where required by law, to protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense. If advertising/analytics tools are used, see our Third Party Online Advertising page.

6) Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described, including fulfilling transactions, providing support, meeting legal/accounting obligations, and resolving disputes. We then delete or anonymize where reasonable.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to reduce risk.

8) International processing

Depending on our hosting and service providers, personal information may be processed outside of Canada. In such cases, it may be subject to the laws of the jurisdiction where it is processed.

9) Access, correction, and questions

You may request access to or correction of your personal information, subject to applicable law and identity verification. To make a request, contact us at info@naturalalternatives.ca.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect changes. Material changes will be highlighted in a reasonable manner where appropriate.
